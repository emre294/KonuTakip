There is a UX improvement needed in the Daily Study Plan.

Do NOT redesign the Study Plan.

Do NOT change notifications.

Do NOT change statistics.

Only adjust the validation rules for the Topic field.

==================================================
CURRENT BEHAVIOR
==================================================

The Topic field is always required.

This includes recurring study sessions ("Repeat Every Day").

This is inconvenient because students usually study different topics each day while keeping the same recurring study habit.

==================================================
EXPECTED BEHAVIOR
==================================================

Validation should depend on the session type.

--------------------------------------------------
One-time Study Session
--------------------------------------------------

Topic field remains REQUIRED.

The student must enter a topic.

Example:

"Functions"

--------------------------------------------------
Recurring Daily Study Session
--------------------------------------------------

Topic field becomes OPTIONAL.

The student may leave it empty.

Example:

Subject:

TYT Mathematics

Topic:

(empty)

Questions:

40

Repeat:

Every Day

This should save successfully.

==================================================
UI
==================================================

Do not remove the Topic field.

Keep it visible.

Simply make it optional for recurring sessions.

If left empty, display a subtle placeholder such as:

"Optional for recurring study plans"

or

"You can study different topics each day."

• One-time study sessions
