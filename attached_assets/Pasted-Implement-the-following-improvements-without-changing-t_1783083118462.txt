5. NOTIFICATION SYSTEM
==================================================

The notification system must be completely reliable.

Notifications are currently not working correctly and must be fixed.

Requirements:

- Every topic must support its own reminder.
- Users must be able to choose reminder intervals of 3, 5 or 7 days.
- Schedule the notification immediately after the interval is selected.
- Deliver notifications exactly on the selected reminder day.
- Notifications must work even if the application is in the background or completely closed.
- Request notification permission automatically when needed.
- If permission is denied, inform the user clearly.
- Store all scheduled notifications permanently so they survive app restarts and device reboots whenever supported.
- Never create duplicate notifications.
- Cancel the notification automatically if the topic is marked as completed or the reminder is removed.
- Tapping a notification must open the application directly to the related topic.
- Configure Android notification channels correctly.
- Follow Expo Notifications best practices.
- Add proper validation, error handling and logging so notification scheduling failures can be detected and fixed.
- Verify that notifications are actually scheduled and delivered successfully.

Example notifications:

📚 Time to review "Functions". Keep your knowledge fresh!

📖 It's been 5 days since your last review of "Cell Division". Let's practice again!

🎯 Don't lose your progress! Review "Probability" today.
